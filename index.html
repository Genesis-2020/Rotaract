    About

    Established in 2007, Rotaract club of GITAM(RACG) is a university based club committed to serve the community through volunteer service projects and social outreach. RACG is one of the 40 clubs in R. I. District 3020, which comprises of six revenue districts viz; Krishna, West Godavari, East Godavari, Visakhapatnam, Vizianagaram & Srikakulam. The Rotaract Club of GITAM works under the guidance of Rotary Club of Visakhapatnam.

    Rotaract stands for "Rotary in Action" and has the following goals:

    • To develop professional and leadership skills.
    • To emphasize respect for the rights of others, and to promote ethical standards and the dignity of all useful occupations.
    • To provide opportunities for young people to address the needs and concerns of the community and our world.
    • To provide opportunities for working in cooperation with sponsoring Rotary clubs.
    • To motivate young people for eventual membership in Rotary.

    Description

    Ingenio 8.0 is a fundraising event for the welfare of various specially-abled schools in Visakhapatnam. This event consists of various activities which helps in the development of different aspects of the participants.

    The fundraising event of the of Ingenio 8.0 is named as “Hrudayankur”, means the seeds of the heart which needs to be nurtured.

    Motive

    The main motive of “Ingenio” is to give a platform to Specially-abled children from different schools in Visakhapatnam to showcase their talents and to enlighten them with joy. The money raised by doing the event will be used to buy some essentials to the Children.

    Fun Activities

    Many fun filled, interactive activities are ready to improve the holistic development of the students. These activities are conducted using various online platforms like WhatsApp, Zoom online meetings, etc.. By participating in these activities, students can get relief form their stress.

    Mock Interviews

    The prime focus of Ingenio 8.0 is Mock Interviews. Interviews are never a cake walk, one must need a prior practice before attending an interview. Since these are mock interviews no one will be discarded at any round. These mock interviews are done in 4 rounds:

    1. Aptitude exam
    2. Group Discussion
    3. Technical Round
    4. HR Round

    Cultural Night

    The cultural night of Rotaract Club of GITAM holds its specialty by giving the platform to specially abled children from different schools in Vizag. This super talented children will show their power packed performances through Zoom online meetings. A part from that, We have performances of our own Rotaractors.
